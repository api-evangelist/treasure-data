Modern data warehouses like Snowflake and BigQuery excel at storing centralized customer data but were not designed to activate it into real-time campaigns and personalized experiences. Treasure AI positions itself as a separate Agentic Experience Platform that sits atop existing warehouses to handle audience creation, journey orchestration, and autonomous decision-making without requiring data migration. The argument is that consolidating data and acting on it are two different jobs, so enterprises need a purpose-built activation layer rather than expecting their warehouse to become a customer data platform.
